Recommendations
4 actions for leadership — prioritised by impact
01
Fix the Replenishment Model
33% low-stock and 37% overstock across ALL categories simultaneously points to a broken reorder formula — not a product or store problem. Define dynamic reorder points using Inventory Level vs Units Sold × 1.5 per category and store. This is the highest-priority fix.
02
Introduce Seasonal Weighting
MAE is flat at 8.32–8.40 across all 4 seasons — the forecast model applies zero seasonal adjustment. Start with Furniture in Autumn and Toys in Winter where error is highest, then expand to all category-season combinations.
03
Review Promotion ROI
Promotions run on 50% of all trading days yet deliver zero incremental demand (137.6 vs 137.8 units). Before the next promotional cycle, conduct a full spend audit across all categories to identify whether any promotion type drives genuine uplift.
04
Address Grocery Underpricing
Groceries average −18.3% below competitor price — significantly exceeding the 15% alert threshold. This affects all regions, suggesting a national pricing policy issue. An immediate pricing review is recommended to recover margin without volume risk.
